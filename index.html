About Us

About Us

Since its opening in 2011, The Heights Dance Studio has become
one of the most prestigious Latin dancing schools in New York City.

Our studio offers many different classes during the week.
So whether you are Beginner, Advanced Beginner, or a more experienced dancer,
we have a place for you.

Styles range from Salsa, Bachata, and Kizomba.
We also offer private lessons and bi-weekly masterclasses and socials.

Group Classes

Our group classes are beginner friendly in a professional environment. We offer Salsa on 2 "New York Style" (Level I & II), Bachata (Level I & II), and Kizomba (Level I & II).

We will go over music, footwork, partnering, shines, styling & spinning techniques. No partner is necessary. Just drop in on the class of your choice.

Private Lessons

Our private lessons are designed to help you dance with confidence. Learn one-on-one with your instructor and get the extra attention and detail you need to take your dancing to the next level! Get all your questions answered during the 50 minutes of private lessons and learn much faster.

Socials & Masterclasses

Our bi-weekly dance socials offer a great atmosphere for students to practice what they've recently learned in class.

We visit local dancing places around our studio to get a "real world" experience on the dance floor. We post the location a week in advance.
